Raiza Goel

Profiler of creative people

Being in the same room with people who inspire you is one of the best things you can do for your dreams. That’s why I try to meet as many creative people as I can and bring their stories to you –

I’ve interviewed over 160 people to date: Oscar winners, an Olympian, New York Times writers, an EGOT winner, Disney legends (like songwriters of Frozen and writer of Little Mermaid), and original cast members of Hamilton.
